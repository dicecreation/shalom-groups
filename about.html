History

Shalom English School has been set up under the aegis of Shalom Educational and Social Welfare Society in the year 2003 to educate the underprivileged children among the Dalit’s, the out caste members in Caste System maintained by the dominant religious groups of India.

In 2003, when the school started the percentage of educated villagers were only 30%. The district comprises of tribal people who did not see the need of education as critical for the development of their community.

The repeated attempt of Pr. Jose Joseph in Pratapgarh produced results when 35 small children got enrolled in the school for the 1st Year. The School started in a rented house in residential area. Within 2012 the strength of the school grew to an outstanding number of 900 children. Five houses were rented, and the strength of the children reached more than 1000 within short span of time.

At present it has 1000 children studying in all grades till english division. The School is equipped with 36 teaching and 20 non-teaching staff. Now it has become a reputed English School in Pratapgarh district of Rajasthan. During the past years of successful voyage it has searched, found and made some distinguishable scholars through academic, spiritual and physical training through proper evaluation.

Facilities

The new attractive building has brightly coloured classrooms and wide, open walkways. Outside, there is a basketball court, a big playground and separate lawned play areas for nursery and junior children. Three spacious Science laboratories have been designed and equipped to match with the best in the country. The school has a fully networked and Wi-Fi enabled campus and computer suite with individual access to internet and email. Additionally, the school has rooms catering for art and craft, music and mathematics.

Transportation

There is a well-organized transport facility in the school. To ensure the wellbeing and safety of students, a fleet of buses ply throughout the city for students’ transportation. Availing of the transport facility is optional.
